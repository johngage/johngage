Center for Smarter Infrastructure

About Us

The Center for Smarter Infrastructure is an experimental research center associated with the UC Berkeley Center for Smart Infrastructure. We are dedicated to pioneering the future of infrastructure through innovative research and development.

Our Research Interests

  • Fiber Optic Sensing: We are exploring the potential of fiber optic sensors in monitoring the structural health of civil infrastructures.
  • Machine Learning in Civil Engineering: We are leveraging machine learning algorithms to predict and analyze infrastructure performance and needs.
  • Cybersecure Civil Engineering Projects: We are working on integrating cybersecurity principles into civil engineering projects to protect infrastructure data and systems.
